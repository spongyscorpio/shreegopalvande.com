Kedarnath - Ek Dham Yatra

5 Nights / 6 Days

5 Nights Itinerary Covering: Delhi - Haridwar - Guptkashi - Gaurikund - Kedarnath - Gaurikund - Guptkashi - Rishikesh - Haridwar - Delhi

Overview

The divine abode of Lord Shiva, Kedarnath Dham is listed among the holiest destinations of Hindu Pilgrimage. One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, the yatra to this dham paves the way to salvation. Devotees from all parts of the globe take part in this yatra. Indian Holiday presents a spiritual Kedarnath Yatra. This journey also includes visit to other holy destinations such as Haridwar, Rishikesh and Guptkashi.

Highlits

  • Enjoy the Ganga Arti (river worship ceremony) at Har Ki Pauri
  • Pay a visit to major attractions of Guptkashi- Vishwanath temple, Ardhnareshwar temple and Manikarnik Kund
  • Attend the ‘Abhishek ceremony’ of Lord Shiva in Kedarnath temple
  • Visit the hanging bridges in Rishikesh- Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula
